# 🧠 Marketing Psychology

Guide customers to your highest-converting bundles.

| Badge | Bundle | Purpose |
|--------|---------|---------|
| ⭐ Chef's Recommendation | Fiesta Signature | Encourage first-time buyers to try your signature products. |
| 🔥 Most Popular | Fiesta Celebration | Uses social proof to increase confidence. |
| 👑 Best Value | Ultimate Fiesta | Highlights the highest-value package for larger gatherings. |

Instead of emphasizing discounts, highlight:

- Homemade quality
- Signature recipes
- Premium ingredients
- Perfect for celebrations
- Great value for groups

Customers remember the experience more than the amount they saved.
